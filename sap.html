MY ROLE

UI/UX Research & Design

CHALLENGE

Create a web application that will allow interviewers to record and transcribe voice recordings, add labels for easy analysis, and provide privacy for certain phrases that are confidential.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

To begin our research, we compiled a competitive analysis to compare existing transcription websites and see what features we can integrate into our web app. We looked extensively into their strengths, weaknesses, and pricing.

USER INTERVIEWS

In addition to the competitive analysis, we furthered our research by conducting user interviews to better understand who are users are.

Target users:
- Graduate students: Transcribing research interviews
- Employees: Transcribing meetings
Goal: Aim to alleviate the pain of manually transcribing audio files and editing the audio to censor sentitive information

Key Findings:
1. UNRELIABLE/RELIABILITY
Users have described experience using voice transcription tools not entirely negative, but could be better. Unreliable--have to go back and manually check transcription, would prefer to be more accurate. Understand challenges with technologies. (50-60% accuracy)
2. SPEED/TIME
Improving productivity and efficiency, readily available. Want to save time (if scheduled to do a lot of interviews (50+)). Used for interviews, voice memo of answers, after surgery couldnt type but needed to type, research (user experiments), analyze conversations, meetings (record voice and take manual notes)
3. ACCURACY
Cannot understand what people are saying in the recording (have to write "inaudible", but AI transcriber is not corrrect when transcribing parts)
4. PAIN
Saves wrist pain/well being
Meetings: a lot of knowledge just talked about, not many people take notes
5. PRIVACY
Issues: no idea what is happening with data (uncomfortable uploading data)

Features:
tagging, putting markers, can go back ad replay, commands and graphical user interface, place visible markers along player, can do it multiple times(be able to play from marker), ability to insert time stamps, to do transcription as you are uploading the file-can listen and correct certain parts, flagging(tools can flag for you "I'm not sure about this part"), specify timestamp of speaker, distinguish different speakers, playback speed, add new speakers, search and replace

USER PERSONAS

Based on the information gathered from our research, we came up with two ideal personas that would be utilizing this transcription tool. Taking their goals, motivations, and frustrations, we used this as a guide in our design process to create a solution in the viewpoint of an ideal user.
