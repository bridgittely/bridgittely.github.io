Background

Every quarter, UC Irvine's Alpha Chi Omega holds a campuswide fundraiser to raise money for the women's shelters in Orange County. During winter quarter, we held an event called "Donut Let Love Hurt," where we had donuts and other sweet treats for our guests as we discussed the importance of having healthy relationships and raised awareness for domestic violence. I created all of the social media fliers to fit the theme that the sorority used to advertise for the event using Adobe Illustrator and Canva.
