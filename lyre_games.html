BACKGROUND

Every quarter, UC Irvine's Alpha Chi Omega holds a campuswide fundraiser to raise money for the women's shelters in Orange County. In spring, we hold our biggest event called "Lyre Games," where we had various fun activities throughout the week for all of the different Greek organizations at school to contribute to our cause. I created all of the social media fliers that the sorority used to advertise for the special event using Adobe Illustrator and Canva.
